## Budget Analysis

### Project Budget Allocation
- **Total Team Budget**: ₱20,000.00
- **Hardware Costs**: ₱16,028.00 (80.14% of budget)
- **Remaining Budget**: ₱3,972.00 (19.86% of budget)

### Remaining Budget Allocation
The remaining ₱3,972.00 will cover:
- Software development tools and subscriptions
- Testing and prototyping materials
- Documentation and printing costs
- Contingency funds for unexpected expenses
- Transportation for field visits and interviews

### Cost Justification
- **Component Selection**: All components chosen are off-the-shelf, readily available, and cost-effective for a prototype system
- **Scalability**: Components can be sourced in bulk for production scaling
- **Quality**: Industrial-grade components ensure reliability for agricultural use
- **Local Availability**: Most components available through Philippine suppliers (Shopee, local electronics shops)

### Return on Investment (ROI) Projection
Based on stakeholder interviews and market research:
- **Target Yield Increase**: 20% improvement in mushroom production
- **Cost Recovery Period**: 6-8 months for small-scale growers
- **Annual ROI**: 150-200% based on increased yields and direct-to-consumer sales
- **Break-even Analysis**: System pays for itself within one growing cycle

### Funding Sources
- **Primary**: Team budget allocation (₱20,000)
- **Secondary**: Potential sponsorships from Department of Agriculture or technology partners
- **Future**: Government grants for agricultural innovation (DA, DTI programs)

## Procurement Timeline
- **Week 1-2**: Core controller (Raspberry Pi) and basic sensors
- **Week 3-4**: Environmental control components (fans, humidifier, filters)
- **Week 5-6**: Structural components (grow tent, mounting hardware)
- **Week 7-8**: Power management and connectivity components

## Risk Mitigation
- **Price Fluctuations**: Budget includes 10% contingency for component price changes
- **Availability**: Multiple suppliers identified for critical components
- **Import Delays**: Local alternatives identified for imported components
- **Currency Exchange**: Budget fixed in Philippine Pesos to avoid exchange rate risks
